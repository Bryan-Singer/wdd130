History

The WWRC found it's home in a nook between the world you call earth and the space between spaces several hundred years ago. It wasn't until recently we were able to create tunnels to other realities safely for long enough that we could open our doors to you and allow other dimensional guests experience just what the multiverse has to offer.

Pictured here, a group of the seventh test run of the Halley's Comet Circuit enjoy the monochrome sights and low gravity thrill of with none of the atmosphere. It was tests like these that paved the road for our 3,427th test to be the first succesful run of an interstellar body. Due to the laws of time between dimensions, we honor their sacrifice everyday.
